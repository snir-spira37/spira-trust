## Bias Safeguards

The authorization correctly prevents a biased comparison by requiring:

```text

no "no competitors" claim
no market-exclusivity claim without evidence

missing documentation not treated as missing capability
```

This is the right guardrail. The future mapping is allowed to discover that SPIRA's positioning is strong, weak, narrow, crowded, or in need of revision.
