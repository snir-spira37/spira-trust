## 1. Purpose

This document authorizes the revised live readiness gate for the accepted
machine-contract passthrough architecture.

The revised readiness question is:

```text
Can SPIRA preserve the authoritative machine contract mechanically,
and can the model explain and obey that contract without contradiction,
unsafe override, unsupported claim, or sensitive-value exposure?
```

The readiness no longer asks the model to regenerate the SPIRA contract as the
source of truth.
